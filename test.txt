### f_exp_jco_1_12m_min_balance_to_turnover

**Prefix:** `f_exp_jco` – expert feature for joint / co-borrower  

**Horizon:** `1_12m` – last 12 months prior to application  

**Metric:** `min_balance_to_turnover`  

**Meaning:**  
This feature compares the **lowest account balance reached during the last 12 months** with the **total transaction turnover over the same period** for the joint/co-borrower.

In simple terms, it shows how small the borrower’s lowest balance was relative to the overall size of their cash flows, helping indicate whether periods of low liquidity occurred despite substantial transaction activity.

### f_exp_jco_1_12m_m_min_balance_min

**Prefix:** `f_exp_jco` – expert feature for joint / co-borrower  

**Horizon:** `1_12m` – last 12 months prior to application  

**Metric:** `m_min_balance`  

**Aggregation:** `min`  

**Meaning:**  
This feature records the **lowest monthly minimum balance observed across all months in the last 12 months** for the joint/co-borrower.

It represents the most stressed balance level reached during the period, without any normalization or scaling.

### f_exp_jco_1_3m_mean_m_min_balance_to_mean_m_turnover

**Prefix:** `f_exp_jco` – expert feature for joint / co-borrower  

**Horizon:** `1_3m` – last 3 months prior to application  

**Metric:** `mean_m_min_balance_to_mean_m_turnover`  

**Meaning:**  
This feature compares the **average of monthly minimum balances** with the **average monthly turnover** over the most recent three months for the joint/co-borrower.

It reflects how the borrower’s typical low balance compares to their typical level of transaction activity, offering a short-term view of liquidity relative to ongoing cash flows.
